The Project

This project has been created for the Laboratory exam of the Master's Degree of Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge, held by Porf. Francesca Tomasi.

The project creates a virtual tour exploring Ireland through the myth of the Banshee, one of the oldest and most known Celtic Myths. The project involves some historical and philological research, the creation of a small 3D model and a sotrytelling section.
